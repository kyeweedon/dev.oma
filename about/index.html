Outboard Motors Australia Pty Ltd is proud to introduce an exciting new range of outboard motors that combine performance with reliability from 3 to 90 HP. Outboard Motors Australia is delivering an interruption strategy to the Australian outboard motor market, to benefit you! Quality product delivered directly to you with free freight!

Outboard Motors Australia Pty Ltd brings to you TITAN, manufactured in a new state of the art manufacturing facility located west of Shanghai; the factory boasts world class machine tools, high pressure die casting, painting and assembly lines. Critical engine rotating and reciprocating elements such as pistons, piston rings, connecting rods and crank shafts are made by specialist manufacturers who also produce these components for the established brands.

Outboard Motors Australia is the exclusive distributor of TITAN in our region. We are creating the opportunity for OWNERS to buy the first credible new outboard motor product in the Australian market in over 30 years. Extensive testing has been undertaken in salt and fresh water in Australian conditions before we have committed to this product.

The 2012 TITAN product offering is based on simple but robust 2-strokes from 3hp to 90hp outboard motors with short and long shaft configuration, remote control and power trim as per the schedule attached. While this initial product offering is with mature technology in reed valve carburetion, the units feature current industry standard safety features such as lanyard emergency stop and in gear starting protection.

This product range also contains work models in 10hp, 18hp and 40hp which feature heavy duty cooling system components and optimised fuel delivery for professional applications. The 2012 TITAN product offering will introduce the four stroke range and the partnership with all units shipped standard with Solas Propellers offering world class products and range.
